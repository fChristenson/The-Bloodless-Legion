# Elix's Extraordinary Armor and Shields

## Description	
The shop is a single storey half-timbered building, with carved wooden doors and dwarf-wrought iron furnishings. It has several chimneys, each exhaling smoke and fiery sparks.

## Shopkeeper	
The shopkeeper is a young female human named Elix. She is a cunning haggler.
